Why Choose Us?

Advanced Laboratory Testing Ltd (ALT) was established in Newbridge, County Kildare in 2013 to provide for the first time, a full range of indigenous laboratory testing services. ALT is a wholly owned IRISH company, strategically located, with a management team of unrivalled experience in Ireland and a dedicated commitment to providing testing services of the highest quality by working in partnership with its customers to ensure that their expectations are met on a continual basis throughout Ireland. We continually strive to improve the range and scope of accreditations and approvals by investing in the most up to date methodologies and technologies, all based on the international laboratory accreditation standard ISO/IEC 17025:2005. We are committed to continual expansion and investment in new technologies, and instrumentation which will allow us to expand our scope of INAB accreditation and approvals in conjunction with customer demand.
